Product Description:

Stay connected to your dog with My Emotion Monitoring Device. This collar allows you to keep a close eye on your beloved pet. Does your dog suffer from a disease or loves to wander out on their own adventures? Worry no more, ME has GPS tracker and monitors heart and activity levels right to the palm of your hands with our application. 10 million pets get lost every year; ME eliminates this problem by using Global Positioning Satellites and breakthrough cellular technology to locate your dog. Notifications and text messages will alert you if your dog has left your set zone. Monitor your dog’s daily activity to ensure they are getting the exercise that they need. ME provides breed(s)-specific guidelines so you can see how other dogs stand compared to other dogs. Share reports with your veterinarian so they know exactly how your dog is doing. Share with the world how wonderful your dog is; we currently support Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. We at ME are Pet Parents and we want to ensure you the require attention your pet deserves

Market

The pet industry is a large and expanding market. The most rapidly growing market segment is pet products and supplies, which represents 5.5% of the market. The segment is projected to increase from $5.2 billion to $6.9 billion in the next five years, according to business Communications Company Pet Industry study. The reason for this tremendous growth in pet products and supplies is the growing number of pet owners and pets.

About The Product Market

Situation Analysis

According to the American Pet Products Association (APPA) there are approximately 80 million dogs in the U.S. alone, with nearly 40% of households owning at least one dog. In fact, there are actually more households in American with dogs than households with kids, so it’s no surprise that we are seeing a growing number of devices aimed at this market.

About The Product Placement

Trends

Human beings are not the only ones donning ever-more powerful wearable electronic devices. Our four-legged friends have an increasing array of smart collars from which to choose. The market for pet products has been growing rapidly with demographics driving the demand for pet supplies. Aging baby boomers are filling their empty nests with pets, while the number of households with children from 5 to 15, the core pet market, keeps climbing as well.
